Abuse policy

What we'll take down

  • Content that violates US law (CSAM, illegal-activity coordination, credible threats, doxxing).
  • Material posted without the subject's consent (private chat logs, leaked credentials, doxxing).
  • DMCA notices that meet the standard requirements.
  • Targeted harassment.

What we won't

  • Take down a page just because it's critical of you, your product, or your work.
  • Reveal who posted a page. (We don't store identifiers, so we couldn't if we tried.)

How to report

Use the form below, or email abuse@share-insights.pages.dev with the URL and reason. Reviewed within 24 hours.

Operator

This service is operated by an individual (not an Anthropic / OpenAI / etc. service). Personally responsible for reviewing reports. The backend stores only the rendered HTML, a timestamp, and a derived (non-reversible) IP hash. There is no per-user storage.
